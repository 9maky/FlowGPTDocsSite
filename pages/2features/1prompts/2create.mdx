## How do I create a prompt on FlowGPT?

To create a prompt on FlowGPT, you will need to follow these steps:

1. Go to the [FlowGPT website](https://flowgpt.com/).
2. Click on the "Create Prompt" button.
3. In the "Prompt" field, type in your prompt.
4. Add name of the prompt and description. You can use Auto Fill if you don't know what to put.
5. In the "Tags" field, you can add tags to your prompt. Tags are used to categorize your prompt and make it easier for other users to find.
6. Click on the "Submit" button.

Your prompt will be created and added to the FlowGPT database. You can then use your prompt to generate text, translate languages, write different kinds of creative content, and answer your questions in an informative way.
