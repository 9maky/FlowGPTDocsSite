## How to share prompts

**Overview**

You can share prompts with other users so that they can use them too. To share a prompt, follow these steps:

1. Find the prompt that you want to share.
2. Click the **Share** button next to the prompt.
3. Select how you want to share the prompt. You can share it on social media or by copying the link.

**Tips**

- You can share multiple prompts at once.
- You can share prompts with other users who are not logged in to FlowGPT.
- You can share prompts with other users who are logged in to FlowGPT.
